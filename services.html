About Us

We Specialize In High Performance Epoxy Flooring Solutions For Residential, Commercial, And Industrial Spaces. Our Services Include Surface Preparation, Prime, Custom Design And Expert Installation, Delivering Durable, Chemical Resistant And Low Maintenance Floors That Enhance Both Function And Appearance. Whether You're Looking For A Sleek Finish For A Showroom Or Heavy-Duty Protection For A Warehouse, We Tailor Every Project To Meet Your Specific Needs. With Our 20+ Years In Knowledge And Experience We Will Customize And Provide Flooring Built To Last. We Also Help Protect And Bring Your Flooring Back To Life With Stone Cleaning & Restoration Which Include Natural Stone; Traventine, Marble, Saltillo. Bringing Back That Natural Shine With A Clean And Seal - Natural Stone Is Common Amongst Many Homes Across The Valley. Due To Natural Stone’s Porous Nature, It Is Recommended That They Be Cleaned Annually To Minimize Bacteria And Dullness.
